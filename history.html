Regional Variations

Different regions of Korea developed their own hanbok styles:

  • Seoul Style: Elegant and refined, with shorter jeogori
  • Jeolla Province: Known for vibrant colors and elaborate decorations
  • Gyeongsang Province: Featured more conservative designs
  • Northern Regions: Practical designs adapted to colder climates

Modern Hanbok Movement

Contemporary hanbok has evolved while preserving traditional elements:

  • Daily Hanbok: Simplified designs for everyday wear
  • Fusion Hanbok: Mixing traditional and Western elements
  • K-Fashion: Influence on global fashion trends
  • Cultural Events: Revival in weddings and festivals

Young designers continue to innovate while respecting traditional craftsmanship and cultural significance.
